## Examples

``` r
## These are equivalent
graph_(cbind(1:5, 2:6), from_edgelist(directed = FALSE))
#> Warning: `graph_()` was deprecated in igraph 2.1.0.

#> IGRAPH ae618ae U--- 6 5 --
```
